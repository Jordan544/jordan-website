ABOUT US

ST. JOSEPH CENTENARY SS-NDEEBA

St. Joseph Centenary Secondary School Ndeeba is a catholic funded school which focuses on bringing up God Fearing, intelligent, and self esteemed students in the society through this we nurture academic excellence, discipline, Leadership and Talented students who are responsible and capable citizens. We provide a conducive environment, have supportive teachers who are qualified , have a well equipped library and Labaratory where all practical studies are conducted.
Our school prepares students to become responsible citizens capable of competing globally.

We provide a conducive learning environment supported by qualified teachers, modern ICT laboratories, a well-equipped library and various co-curricular activities that promote holistic development.

OUR VISION

To be a leading center of educational excellence that empowers students to innovate, lead, and positively impact a rapidly changing global society.

OUR MISSION

To produce higly intelligent student capable of proper decision making towards generally occuring problems in the modern world.
